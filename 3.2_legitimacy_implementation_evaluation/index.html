Policy Legitimation

Policy Legitimation

  • Giving legal force to decisions
  • Authorizing or justifying policy action

Two perspectives:

  1. Simple: Approval by recognized authority
  2. Complex: Multiple conditions for acceptability

Complex Legitimation

Requires more than majority vote or legal sanction:

  • Consistent with Constitution or existing law
  • Compatible with U.S. political culture and values
  • Demonstrable popular support

Consequences of Lack of Legitimation

  • Failure to command public support
  • Opposition from affected interest groups
  • Potential legal challenges
  • Adverse effects on implementation

Policy Implementation

Policy Implementation

  • Translating decisions into action
  • Key components:
    • Organization: Resources & procedures
    • Interpretation: Administrative discretion
    • Application: Service delivery
  • Aligns with Three Streams model

Importance of Implementation

  • 'Missing link' in policy process
  • Government in action via executive branch
  • Shapes outcomes more than design
  • Reflects punctuated equilibrium in practice

Policy Evaluation

Policy Evaluation

  • Assesses output and outcome goal achievement
  • Focuses on:
    • Effectiveness
    • Efficiency
    • Equity
    • Legitimacy
    • Administrative feedback

Reasons for Evaluation

  • Stakeholder accountability
  • Administrative oversight
  • Identifies change opportunities
  • Enables continuous improvement

Public Administrators' Role

Public Administrators' Role

  • Street-level bureaucrats: Public administrators who directly interact with citizens and implement policies at the local level.
  • Accountability agents: Individuals or organizations responsible for ensuring that public administrators are held accountable for their actions and decisions.
  • Feedback providers: Stakeholders who provide feedback on policy implementation, helping to identify areas for improvement.
  • Complexity navigators: Public administrators who navigate complex policy environments and manage the intricacies of policy implementation.
